Overview

Purpose

Have a compelling online presence that makes us the go-to rafting company
for experienced water rafters, but approachable for newcomers.
Customers shouldn't feel like they need to do any additional research
after viewing this site.

Audience

Our target audience are experienced water rafters, as well as newcomers looking for a positive first experience.
What do they want? They want to know that they will have a positive experience with a professional company at an affordable price
What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They expect to find pricing, excursion offerings and information, contact information, customer testimonials, etc.
How will my audience usually access my site? Primarily via mobile device, but through large form factor devices as well.
